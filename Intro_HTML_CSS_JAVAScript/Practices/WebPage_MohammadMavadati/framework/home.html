Bio:

Mohammad Mavadati received his PhD degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from University of Denver in January 2015. His PhD research focused on spontaneous facial expression computing and human's behavior analysis. In Febuary 2015 he joined Affectiva , the leading emotion sensing company, and as a Computer Vision Scientist he is developing computer vision and machine learning techniques for automatic facial expression and emotion recognition. His career interests and goals lie in computer vision and machine learning, Emotion Analysis and Human-Machine Interaction.
